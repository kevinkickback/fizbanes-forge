Tooltip System Test Page

Testing Instructions:

  • Hover: Move your mouse over any highlighted reference to show tooltip
  • Pin (Ctrl+P): Press Ctrl+P while hovering to pin a tooltip - it should stay visible
  • Copy (Ctrl+C): Press Ctrl+C to copy tooltip content to clipboard
  • Close (Esc): Press Escape to close the active tooltip
  • Action Buttons: Look for pin, copy, and close icons in the top-right of tooltips
  • Navigation: After pinning, move to another reference - pinned tooltip should remain
